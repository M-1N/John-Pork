The investigation of John Pork

On Oct. 2, 2005, I stumbled upon a photo in the woods near a trail on Belle Isle, Richmond, VA. The photo that was found depicted these unidentifiable blue objects or “UFOs,” as enthusiasts of the supernatural and the extraterrestrial would say. Personally, I’m not a firm believer of these kinds of things, but this photo piqued my interest. In turn, I found it fitting to document my investigation into this photo.
